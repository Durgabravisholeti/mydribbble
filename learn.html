Join thousands of graduates and land your dream design role at companies like;

Build UX/UI foundations and more

This course will teach you design thinking approaches and how to implement them, how to conduct and analyze UX/UI research to inform your design decisions, and how to create wireframes, flow diagrams, and prototypes to bring your product design projects to life.

You'll master Figma's tools, libraries, systems, and techniques, and receive guidance on resumes, portfolios, and interviews to help you land your dream job.

Syllabus Highlights

24 weeks of training and mentorship

Weeks 1 - 4

Design Thinking, UX Fundamentals, User Flows & Wireframing

Weeks 5 - 9

Visual Design, Design Systems, and Components

Weeks 10 - 11

Prototyping, Testing, Documentation, & Handoff

Weeks 12 - 15

Students work on completing their Capstone Project

Weeks 17 - 20

Portfolio development & defining the right role for you

Weeks 21 - 24

Finding jobs, interview prep, salary negotiations, & goal-setting.
